*DIGS* is a highly intuitive and powerful Ground Control Station (GCS) designed for UAVs. Whether you're a first-time pilot or an experienced professional, DIGS provides a seamless user experience for flight control and mission planning, making it the go-to solution for any *MAVLink-enabled drone*.

---

### *Why Choose DIGS?*

- *Ease of Use*: A beginner-friendly interface designed for smooth operation without sacrificing advanced features for pros.
- *Comprehensive Flight Control*: Full flight control and mission management for *PX4* and *ArduPilot* powered UAVs.
- *Mission Planning*: Easily plan complex missions with a simple drag-and-drop interface.

---

### *Key Features*

- *Full Flight Control*: Supports all *MAVLink drones*.
- *Vehicle Setup*: Tailored configuration for *PX4* and *ArduPilot* platforms.
- *Fully Open Source*: Customize and extend the software to suit your needs.

---

### *Get Involved!*

DIGS is *open-source*, meaning you have the power to shape it! Whether you're fixing bugs, adding features, or customizing for your specific needs, DIGS welcomes contributions from the community.
